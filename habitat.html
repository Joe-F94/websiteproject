Sholazar Basin

The Sholazar Basin is a beautiful tropical jungle in the midst of Northrend.Much like Kalimdor's Un'Goro Crater, the Sholazar Basin is an anomaly. It fills the northwest corner of the Borean Tundra, and is surrounded by nasty ridge, requiring climbing to reach it. Loquenahak stalks this jungle and is rarely seen by those seeking it. It has been spotted in many locations and if you also wish to seek this creature you may find more information if you click here.

Storm Peaks

The Storm Peaks is a frigid mountainous region home to many dangerous foe including Frost Giants.Skoll has dens dotted around the region and if you feel like traversing the peaks click here for a heads up where to find him.

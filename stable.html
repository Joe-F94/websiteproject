My Pets

This is Katerina, she is the first rare pet I ever tracked down in the distant shores of Northrend, she likes to eat fish and raw meat and enjoys prowling the lush jungles of Strangle Thorn and Sholazar Basin. She is a dangerous hunter who stalks from the shadows and never misses her prey.

This is Skoll.Skoll is a large blue saber worg that is a rare spawn of Storm Peaks.
Skoll is known for the lightning effect that crackles around his body and his ferocious howl which echoes between the peaks. He enjoyd eating sausages and playing fetch with Goblin heads.

This is Skye, She is a frostsaber spirit beast that wanders The Regrowth in Mount Hyjal.She is incredibly quick and nimble and often brings me small critters to make soups and stews while we are on a hunt.
